Who We Are

Get to know Behaviour, Intelligence & Competency Managers (BICM)

OUR STORY

Empowering Organizations Through Strategic Solutions

Behaviour, Intelligence and Competency Managers (BICM) is a leading Malawian-owned company devoted to Human Resource Training, Education and Development, as well as provision of Management Services, Business Consulting and Organisation Development initiatives. Our focus and pride on the planning, development, budgeting, implementation, monitoring and evaluation of contemporary and strategic policies, procedures, systems and frameworks that are in line with the operational objectives of our esteemed clients.

Our Mission

To provide impactful, value-driven solutions that empower organizations and individuals to thrive through capacity building, strategic planning, and innovative consulting services.

Our Vision

To be the world class provider of socio-economic innovations, training & education, and entrepreneurship services to our esteemed clients.
